# Ny tovona *dis-*

fisarahana,fanaparitahana  :

- *__dis__ĵeti* – manasaritaka zavatra, manaparitaka
- *__dis__sendi* –mampahafantatra zavatra

- *du__on__o*   – an-tsasany
- *tri__on__o*  – ampahatelony
- *kvar__on__o* – ampahaefany
